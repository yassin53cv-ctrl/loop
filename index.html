The House of VESTRA

Craft that endures.
Elegance that speaks.

VESTRA was founded on a singular principle: that true luxury is not about volume or velocity, but about the intimate relationship between a garment and the person who wears it. Each piece is a considered statement, a whisper of extraordinary craft.

We source only the most exceptional fabrics — hand-loomed silk from Piedmont, worsted wool from Scottish highland mills, cashmere combed from Mongolian herds — and entrust them to artisans who have spent lifetimes perfecting their craft.

This is not fashion. This is heritage in motion.

— The VESTRA Atelier
